Leroy Sane, Future Uncertain?

The saga never ends! Just a week or so after reports claimed that Leroy Sané had a deal agreed with Bayern Munich, Bild reporter Christian Falk now says that the club could wait until next year to get him if Manchester City refuse to play ball. While Bayern remains fixated on a player who smashed his ACL to bits and hasn’t played a game in the top flight in almost a year, talents like Kai Havertz and Timo Werner prepare to move across Europe. This is just not a good situation to be in. Hopefully the club gets the transfer done sooner rather than later.

Sky Sport’s resident transfer expert Marc Behrenbeck had this to say on his program, “Transfer Update - die Show” (great German title, I know): “Everything indicates that Leroy Sane is coming. Even if Oliver Kahn took his foot off the gas a little on Sky90,” referring to Kahn’s latest comments that he would not rule out making no major signings this summer. “That notwithstanding,” Behrenbeck continued, “everything indicates that it’s going to happen. It’s only about the transfer fee now.” Well, we’ll see, won’t we?
